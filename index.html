Pantomine – Color Vomit with a Story – 2nd Place Winner – Kent Hack Enough

by Chris | Dec 30, 2014 |

Pantomine – Color Vomit with a Story – 2nd Place Winner – Kent Hack Enough Pantomine is a color selection app that will help you exactly match any color value you want with any camera equiped smart phone or tablet. The way this app works is that you take a picture of the color you are trying to match. Our app finds the exact about our red, green, and blue, given off by a selected pixel in an image source. Then it goes to a large Pantone library and compares this value against it to find the closest match available. It is not an precise instrument yet. Development is being done to color collect for different lenses and lighting conditions. And we are developing an algorythm that more exaclty selects the pixels with the value you seek.
